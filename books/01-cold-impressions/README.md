## Synopsis

When Sarah Carpenter disappears from a rural Indiana gas station, FBI agents Gray and Ellis uncover a case that hits too close to home for Brandon Gray. A deputy sheriff with a childhood obsession, a stepfather trying to protect, and a small-town tragedy that exposes the long reach of trauma.

## Key Themes

- Trauma freezing emotional development
- Obsession vs. love
- Duty vs. self-preservation
- The weight of knowing vs. the burden of proof
